## Content Quality Issues

### Issue 1 — CRITICAL: Product descriptions not crawlable by AI

The product page for the Duotone Dice SLS 2025 renders its description via JavaScript. AI training crawlers and many AI search bots do not execute JavaScript. The full product description — including features, specifications, materials, and riding style guidance — is invisible to these systems. This affects every product page on the site.

**Impact:** AI platforms cannot read, understand, or cite any product-specific content. The site is effectively invisible to AI for product queries despite carrying premium inventory.

**Fix:** Ensure all product description content is present in server-rendered HTML (visible in raw page source without JavaScript execution). On Spree Commerce, this requires ensuring the product description field is rendered in the initial HTML response, not loaded asynchronously.

---

### Issue 2 — HIGH: About Us page is generic and has no experience content

The About Us page is 320 words and contains no information that distinguishes this team from any other water sports retailer. The exact phrase "Our team consists of active water sports enthusiasts who can help you find the right gear" appears word-for-word identical to language used by dozens of other action sports retailers.

**Problematic passages:**

- "We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality water sports equipment at fair prices." — No unique viewpoint, no evidence, no specifics.
- "Whether you are a beginner looking for your first kite or an experienced rider searching for the latest pro gear, we are here to help you." — Could be written by any retailer anywhere.
- "Our team consists of active water sports enthusiasts" — No names, no disciplines, no locations, no years riding.

**Fix:** Rewrite the About Us page (target 600-900 words) to include: founder name and story, year the first product was sold, specific disciplines the team members ride, locations where they session, specific milestones (first brand partnership, 1,000th customer, expansion to new countries), and honest reflection on what 20+ years in this specific market has taught them.

---

### Issue 3 — HIGH: Zero author attribution across entire site

Not one page on surf-store.com attributes content to a named person. This is a significant E-E-A-T weakness. AI platforms cannot assign expertise signals to a source with no identifiable human author.

**Fix:** At minimum, add a "Team" section to the About Us page with 3-5 staff profiles (name, riding discipline, years experience, photo). For any advisory or guide content added in the future, attribute it to a named team member.

---

### Issue 4 — HIGH: Category page buying guide is generic and brief

The entire kitesurfing category editorial section is approximately 200 words. The single practical recommendation ("Beginners should look for a stable, forgiving kite (9–12m depending on weight)") is the most specific content on the page. The rest is marketing copy ("one of the most complete in Europe," "fast shipping").

**Fix:** Expand to a genuine buying guide (800-1,200 words) with sections covering: how to choose kite size by rider weight and wind range, discipline overview (freeride vs. freestyle vs. big air vs. wave vs. foil), key equipment checklist for beginners, progression path from trainer kite to full setup, and seasonal considerations for European water sports. Each section should reflect actual team experience and include specific product recommendations from the store's inventory.

---

### Issue 5 — MEDIUM: "Since 2003" and "+6000 Customers" are unverified claims

These longevity and social proof claims appear on every page but are presented without any supporting evidence. No founding story, no customer testimonials, no review count from a third-party platform.

**Fix:** Add 6-8 real customer testimonials (verbatim, with name/location/sport) to the About Us page and homepage. Integrate with Trustpilot, Google Reviews, or equivalent to provide verifiable third-party ratings. Display the review count and score visibly.

### Issue 6 — MEDIUM: No terms of service or refund policy pages accessible

Both /policies/terms-of-service and /policies/refund-policy return 404 errors. These are required for EU e-commerce compliance (EU Consumer Rights Directive) and are E-E-A-T trust signals.

**Fix:** Create dedicated policy pages at standard URLs. The return policy referenced in marketing copy ("30-day free returns") needs a complete policy document explaining conditions, process, who pays return shipping, and timelines for refunds.

---

### Issue 7 — MEDIUM: No phone number listed anywhere

Email-only contact reduces trust score for customers and AI assessors evaluating business legitimacy.

**Fix:** Add a phone number to the About Us page and footer contact section.

---

### Issue 8 — LOW: No publication or update dates on product pages

The About Us page displays "Updated at: 30.03.2026" which is good practice. Product pages and category pages show no dates, making freshness impossible to assess.

**Fix:** Add visible last-updated dates to category editorial sections and ensure structured data dateModified is populated on product pages.

## Improvement Recommendations

### Quick Wins (implement within 30 days)

1. **Ensure product descriptions render in server-side HTML.** This is the single highest-impact technical fix. Confirm that Spree's product description field is output in the initial server response. Test by viewing page source (Ctrl+U) — if the description text is not visible there, it is JavaScript-rendered and invisible to most AI crawlers.

2. **Add a phone number to the footer and About Us page.** One line of copy, immediate trust uplift.

3. **Create /policies/refund-policy and /policies/terms-of-service pages** at standard URLs with complete content. These are required by EU Consumer Rights Directive anyway.

4. **Add real customer testimonials with names and sports to the About Us page.** Even 4-5 verbatim quotes with "- Marco, kiteboarder, Italy" style attribution dramatically improve social proof and AI citability.

5. **Add explicit authorized dealer language** to the About Us page: "Surf-store.com is an authorized dealer of Duotone, ION, Cabrinha, Nobile, Neilpryde, Fanatic, JP Australia, Tabou, Gaastra, and Point7." This is factual, specific, and highly citable.

### Content Gaps (implement within 90 days)

6. **Rewrite the About Us page** to 600-900 words with: a named founder or team lead, a founding story with specific milestones, staff profiles with riding disciplines and locations, and honest perspective on what 20 years in this market means in practice. Target one memorable, quotable paragraph that AI will extract when answering "who is surf-store.com?"

7. **Expand each category page buying guide** to 800-1,200 words. The kitesurfing guide should cover: kite size selection by weight and wind, discipline types, equipment checklist, beginner progression path, common mistakes, and seasonal tips. Attribute the guide to a named team member.

8. **Create product comparison content** for flagship items. A page titled "Duotone Dice SLS vs Evo SLS 2025: Which Kite Is Right for You?" — written from real riding knowledge — would generate organic AI citations for high-intent product queries.

9. **Launch a content hub** (blog or guides section) with 6-8 articles covering core query types: "How to Choose Your First Kite," "Best Kitesurfing Spots in Slovenia and the Adriatic," "Wing Foil vs Kitesurfing: Which Should You Start With?" This establishes topical authority and gives AI platforms something to cite.

### Author and E-E-A-T Improvements (implement within 60 days)

10. **Create staff profiles with photos.** Even a single named "gear expert" with a 100-word bio, riding history, and photo transforms the experience and expertise scores. This person should be attributed on all buying guides and category content.

11. **Embed Instagram content on the About Us page.** The @surfstore_com account almost certainly contains authentic experience signals — team riding photos, gear reviews, event attendance — that would dramatically strengthen the Experience dimension. An embedded feed or curated gallery converts existing social content into on-site E-E-A-T signals.

12. **Pursue authorized dealer badges from brand partners.** Many brands (Duotone, Cabrinha) provide dealer locator listings or badge programs. Being listed on the official Duotone dealer page with a backlink is a high-authority inbound signal.

13. **Register on Google Business Profile and Trustpilot.** Verified third-party reviews on these platforms are referenced by AI platforms when assessing business credibility. A 4.5+ star rating on Trustpilot with 100+ reviews would be among the most efficient trust investments available.
